A little about us.

<%block name="body_section_content">

As a family owned operation based out of Utah Valley, we at The Family Oriented Music Organization have been meeting customer needs for over half a century. Founded in 1952, the primary focuses of our organization has been renting/selling instruments and accessories, hosting venues, and providing musical services. We have always prided ourselves on delivering value to our customers through an outstanding in-store experience. Throughout the years we have centered our core beliefs around bettering the community, making musical opportunities accessible to everyone, and exceeding customer needs and expectations.
